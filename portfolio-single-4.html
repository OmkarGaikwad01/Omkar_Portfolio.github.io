Flow Dynamics Fusion: A Multiphase Simulation Odyssey

Project Description

Simulation Expertise: Demonstrateed my mastery in transient simulations by applying advanced techniques to model multiphase flow scenarios involving methane and engine oil. Showcased ability to accurately simulate dynamic interactions between different phases. Boundary Conditions Mastery: Highlighted my adeptness in setting boundary conditions crucial for open environment modeling in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations. Showcased skill in defining pressure outlets and wall boundaries to ensure realistic simulation results. Mesh Resolution and Time Step Optimization: Illustrated understanding of mesh resolution and time step size selection to balance accuracy and computational efficiency in simulations. Showcased my expertise in optimizing mesh resolution (e.g., 0.20 cm) and time step size (e.g., 0.004 s) to achieve reliable results. Material Property Handling: Emphasized proficiency in handling material properties essential for engine oil simulations. Showcased my ability to define properties such as density, viscosity, and surface tension coefficient using Fluent database, ensuring accuracy in simulation models. Visualization Proficiency: Highlighted capability to create informative visualizations to effectively communicate simulation results. Showcased skills in generating contour plots, iso-surface representations, and other visualization techniques to provide insights into complex multiphase flow phenomena. Analysis and Interpretation: Illustrated ability to analyze simulation data and draw meaningful conclusions. Showcased skills in interpreting parameters like methane volume fraction and D-index to gain insights into the impact of fresh air injection on reducing the D-index over time. Problem-Solving Skills: Demonstrated problem-solving approach in addressing complex multiphase flow phenomena encountered during simulations. Showcased ability to navigate challenges and make informed engineering decisions based on simulation outcomes. Technical Competence: Highlighted competence in using computational tools like Fluent for CFD simulations. Showcased understanding of multiphase flow dynamics and your ability to apply theoretical concepts to real-world engineering problems.
